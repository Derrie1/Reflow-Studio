Our Story

BORN ON
THE STREET.

Smash started as a single food truck on the streets of Salisbury in 2019. We were obsessed with one thing: the perfect smash burger. Thin patty, maximum crust, real beef flavour. Five years later, we've got three spots — but the obsession hasn't changed.

Every patty is smashed to order. Every bun is toasted. Every sauce is made fresh. We don't cut corners because our food doesn't let us.
